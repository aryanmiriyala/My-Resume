## Purpose

Texas McCombs is seeking a motivated and skilled Software Engineer with experience developing business applications in either the Salesforce Platform ecosystem (Salesforce, Apex, MuleSoft, FormAssembly) or the Microsoft application development ecosystem (.NET, SQL Server, Azure, SSIS, Power BI). This position reports to the Director of Applications and Data and serves as a member of cross-functional teams supporting faculty, staff, and students at the McCombs School of Business.

The Software Engineer will participate in the design, development, testing, integration, deployment, and ongoing support of software applications, data solutions, and business processes. The ideal candidate possesses strong software development fundamentals, a willingness to learn new technologies, and the ability to collaborate effectively with technical and non-technical stakeholders.

The work involves understanding business needs; eliciting, documenting, and clarifying requirements; designing, developing, and testing applications and databases; integrating custom, commercial, and software-as-a-service solutions; and supporting continuous improvement of business processes. Strong communication skills, customer service orientation, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities are highly valued.
