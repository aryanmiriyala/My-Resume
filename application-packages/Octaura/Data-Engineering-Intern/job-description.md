## Company and Market Context

Octaura is a technology company transforming syndicated loan and collateralized loan obligation markets. The company is applying AI, real-time data, and modern software design to markets where workflows still depend heavily on phone calls, email, and PDFs. Its platform combines trading capabilities, data, and analytics to make these markets more transparent, efficient, and connected.

Octaura describes its culture as collaborative, transparent, impactful, innovative, and values-driven. Interns work alongside experienced colleagues in an open office, interact with department heads, C-suite leaders, and clients, and contribute to meaningful projects in a fast-paced startup environment.

## Opportunity

Octaura is seeking motivated summer interns for its data engineering team. Interns will work directly with senior data engineers, software engineers, and data scientists to build and maintain infrastructure and data pipelines that touch all parts of the company.

The role includes developing Python-based data workflows and supporting cloud-based data processing and storage. The data systems support a customer-facing trading platform, internal business intelligence, and agentic AI systems.

Common technologies include:

- Python
- Spark
- Redshift
- Apache Airflow
- Terraform
- AWS Cloud tools

The ideal candidate has a computer science or related background, some experience using SQL and Python or a related language, and a strong interest in building data systems for a customer-facing trading platform, internal business intelligence, and agentic AI systems.
