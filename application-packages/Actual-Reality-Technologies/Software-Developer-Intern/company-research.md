# Company Research

Company: Actual Reality Technologies

Website: https://actualreality.tech/

Date researched: 2026-07-15

## Summary

Actual Reality Technologies presents itself as an enterprise AI consultancy focused on scaling autonomous operations for modern businesses. Its public site emphasizes Industry 5.0, artificial intelligence, machine learning, Lean Six Sigma, app development, predictive maintenance, smart factory implementation, data analytics, workflow automation, and custom AI/LLM agents.

## What They Do

- Enterprise AI consulting and implementation.
- Custom AI solutions, including LLM agents, mobile factory apps, legacy system integration, and workflow automation.
- Data analytics for predictive maintenance, real-time dashboards, supply-chain optimization, and automated reporting.
- AI training and consulting, including AI-era upskilling, Lean Six Sigma plus AI workshops, prompt engineering for teams, and change management.
- Software-development acceleration, including AI-assisted coding tools, automated code review, automated testing/deployment, CI/CD, and traceability-focused safety-critical development workflows.
