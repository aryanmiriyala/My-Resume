# Intuit Company Research - Software Engineer 1

Research checked on 2026-07-15 using official Intuit sources.

## Mission and Customer Context

- Intuit describes its mission as powering prosperity for people and communities.
- Its official company page states that TurboTax, Credit Karma, QuickBooks, and Mailchimp serve approximately 100 million customers worldwide.
- Intuit says these products help consumers and small businesses save time, reduce work, keep more money, and make financial decisions with confidence.

Source:

- https://www.intuit.com/company/

## Operating Values

Intuit's official operating-values page identifies:

- Customer Obsession
- Stronger Together
- Courage
- Integrity Without Compromise
- We Care and Give Back

The page connects these values to customer outcomes, collaboration, bold thinking, inclusion, community prosperity, and ethical conduct.

Source:

- https://www.intuit.com/company/operating-values/
