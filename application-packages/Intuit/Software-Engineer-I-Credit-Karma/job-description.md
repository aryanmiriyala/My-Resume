## Company Overview

Intuit is the global financial technology platform that powers prosperity for the people and communities it serves. With approximately 100 million customers worldwide using products such as TurboTax, Credit Karma, QuickBooks, and Mailchimp, Intuit believes everyone should have the opportunity to prosper and keeps working to find new, innovative ways to make that possible.

## Job Overview

This role is ideal for those at the start of their professional journey, with less than two years of industry experience, who are eager to learn, grow, and make an immediate impact.

Intuit Credit Karma's Recs Platform team is looking for a Software Engineer. The team focuses on delivering personalized financial recommendations that empower millions of members to make informed financial decisions. The team develops state-of-the-art recommendation platforms, including deep learning-based retrieval and ranking systems for personalized recommendations.

The role works on building and optimizing the core services that power these recommendations, collaborating closely with software engineers, data scientists, and data engineers. The position can make a tangible impact on how millions of people manage their financial lives.

## Responsibilities

- Core Product Contribution: Contribute to core products and services, as well as systems that power critical engineering operations. See work launched and help solve meaningful problems for customers.
- Application Development: Gain hands-on experience developing applications using languages such as Java, Python, or Node.js.
- Data and API Integration: Work with database technologies such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB and apply RESTful API design principles to integrate services effectively.
- Engineering Best Practices: Learn and apply proven best practices in designing, building, and supporting software projects while utilizing modern tools like Git, Jenkins, or Travis.
- Team Collaboration: Participate in Agile methodologies, work effectively within Scrum teams, and collaborate closely with peers across disciplines to build strong, supportive working relationships.
- Mentorship and Growth: Receive hands-on guidance, detailed feedback, and mentorship from experienced team members to help launch a rewarding career.

## Qualifications

- Education: Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, a related technical field, or equivalent practical experience.
- Experience: 0-2 years of experience developing software applications; relevant coursework, internships, and academic or personal projects are welcome.
- Technical Fundamentals: Foundational understanding of object-oriented design, programming principles, and Agile development methodologies.
- Programming Languages: Hands-on or academic experience with one or more programming languages, such as Java, Python, C++, etc.
- Emerging Tech Awareness: Awareness of AI concepts and a basic understanding of capabilities like Generative AI.
- Soft Skills: Strong written, verbal, and collaboration skills with the ability to work effectively in a team environment.
- Mindset: Willingness to learn, adapt to new priorities, tools, and technologies in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
