# Data Engineer - SpaceXAI

Location: Palo Alto, CA

SpaceXAI's mission is to create AI systems that can accurately understand the universe and aid humanity in its pursuit of knowledge. The team is small, highly motivated, hands-on, and focused on engineering excellence, initiative, prioritization, and concise communication.

## About the Role

The Data team ensures models are trained on the right data, in the right form, and at the right quality throughout the training lifecycle. The team partners with acquisition, ML, and software engineering groups to identify data needs and build production systems that transform raw inputs into high-quality training data at scale.

As a Data Engineer / AI Engineer, the role develops the systems, processes, and production code for data acquisition, preparation, quality evaluation, and delivery. The ideal candidate combines software engineering fundamentals and coding practices with intuition for statistics, neural networks, and the relationship between data quality and model outcomes.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze the performance and impact of data throughout the model training lifecycle.
- Investigate anomalous model behavior and identify data issues that cause poor downstream performance.
- Design and improve data cleaning, transformation, and quality-control steps for training data.
- Research and develop methods for improving data quality and effectiveness in AI model development.
- Apply statistical techniques and empirical analysis to guide decisions about dataset quality and model outcomes.
- Partner across teams to identify data needs and high-impact acquisition opportunities.
- Build and maintain production-grade pipelines, tooling, and software systems that ingest, process, validate, and deliver training data.
- Develop metrics, evaluation frameworks, and monitoring systems for data quality and model behavior at scale.
- Fuse multiple sources into reliable datasets for research and production model training.
- Create shared datasets, tooling, and internal data products for model analysis and debugging.

## Basic Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in computer science, data science, physics, mathematics, or another STEM discipline.
- 1+ years of data or software engineering experience; internship experience applies.
- Experience implementing or analyzing language models or neural networks.

## Preferred Skills and Experience

- Professional analytics, data science, machine learning, or data engineering experience.
- Production data pipelines for neural-network or large-scale machine-learning workloads.
- Strong Python experience and familiarity with the modern machine-learning and data ecosystem.
- Parquet or similar columnar storage formats in large-scale data systems.
- Kubernetes and distributed production environments.
- Predictive models and machine-learning pipelines, including clustering, forecasting, anomaly detection, or related techniques.
- Terabyte- to petabyte-scale data systems.
- Strong statistical intuition, quantitative analysis, and familiarity with scaling-ladder design studies.
- Effectiveness in a dynamic environment with changing priorities and requirements.
- Ownership of ambiguous problems and ability to drive projects independently.
