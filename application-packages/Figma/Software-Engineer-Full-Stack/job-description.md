# Figma - Software Engineer, Full Stack

Company: Figma  
Role: Software Engineer, Full Stack  
Locations: San Francisco, CA; New York, NY; United States remote  
Employment type: Full-time

## Job Description

Figma is growing its team of creatives and builders on a mission to make design accessible to all. Figma's platform helps teams bring ideas to life, whether they are brainstorming, creating a prototype, translating designs into code, or iterating with AI. Figma empowers teams to streamline workflows, move faster, and work together in real time from anywhere in the world.

As a Full Stack Engineer, the role builds both products and platforms that empower millions of users, from startups to Fortune 500 companies, to create and collaborate in real time. Work may include core features, developer workflows, enterprise infrastructure, new products, intuitive user experiences, performance optimization in real-time collaborative systems, and secure, reliable, scalable systems supporting Figma's growth.

Engineers partner closely with Product, Design, Research, and Data to deliver best-in-class experiences. Openings span teams including Figma Editor, FigJam, Figma Slides, Figma Sites, Figma Buzz, Growth and Monetization, Internal Tools, and cross-product Foundations.

This is a full-time role that can be held from one of Figma's US hubs or remotely in the United States.

## What You'll Do

- Design, build, and ship high-quality features across the stack, from polished front-end experiences to scalable back-end systems.
- Contribute to both new product development and established product improvements, balancing user experience craft with performance and architecture quality.
- Own projects end-to-end, including ideation, technical design, implementation, testing, launch, and iteration.
- Collaborate with Product, Design, Research, and Data to shape product direction and strategy.
- Operate, monitor, and continuously improve systems with a focus on reliability, security, and scalability.
- Mentor teammates, contribute to team culture, and share knowledge through code reviews, design critiques, and pairing.

## Qualifications

- Proven experience shipping and maintaining full-stack applications in production.
- Strong proficiency in modern front-end frameworks such as React/TypeScript.
- Strong proficiency in back-end technologies such as Ruby, Python, Go, C++, and PostgreSQL.
- Product mindset and ability to translate user needs into scalable technical solutions.
- Excellent collaboration and communication skills across cross-functional teams.
- Passion for engineering craft and building polished, maintainable, scalable systems.

## Nice To Have

- Experience using AI-assisted development tools such as GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT, or Claude to write, debug, and optimize code.
- Familiarity with C++ and WebAssembly in user-facing applications.
- Background in growth engineering, developer tooling, billing and payments infrastructure, or extensibility platforms such as plugins, APIs, and widgets.
- Exposure to Stripe, Salesforce, Snowflake, SCIM, SAML, experimentation, or observability tooling.
- Experience leading large-scale projects or launching 0-to-1 products, mentoring peers, and shaping engineering culture.

## Compensation / Logistics

- Annual base salary range for San Francisco or New York hubs: $153,000-$376,000 USD.
- Remote roles are localized between 80% and 100% of hub range.
- If hired, in-person onboarding is required.
