# Jane Street Software Engineering Intern Application Questions

## Extra

Question: Why you are interested in Jane Street, how you heard about Jane Street, and if you have a job now, why you are looking for a new one.

Answer:

What stands out to me about Jane Street is how seriously the internship treats engineering. The projects are not toy assignments, and the work seems to sit close to real systems where correctness, maintainability, and judgment matter. I have not used OCaml professionally yet, but learning it in an environment where people use it every day to build production software is a big part of the appeal.

I first heard about Jane Street through a professor and friends who were interested in quantitative research and technical interviews. The company kept coming up as a place where people work on unusually hard problems and care a lot about the quality of the code behind them. After reading more about the software engineering internship, the mentorship model and project pairing process made it feel like the right kind of stretch.

I am currently interning at SmartSolve, and I am grateful for the experience I am getting there. I am looking for my next internship because I want a role that pushes my software engineering depth further, especially around systems, programming fundamentals, and learning from engineers who have very high standards for production code.
