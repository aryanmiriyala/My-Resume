I would bring Jane Street strong Python and SQL experience, comfort learning from unfamiliar datasets, and a habit of communicating what I find instead of only showing the code. I do not know everything this role will require, and that is part of why it interests me. The chance to own real projects while learning from full-time Data Engineers is exactly the kind of environment where I think I would grow quickly and contribute carefully.
