## Role Summary

The AI Engineer will build and refine autonomous AI agents, develop prompt engineering workflows, and integrate Large Language Models into the Jobright application.

## Responsibilities

- Build and refine autonomous AI agents that interact with complex environments to solve real-world user problems.
- Develop and optimize prompt engineering workflows and multi-agent orchestration frameworks to enhance product capabilities.
- Integrate Large Language Models into the Jobright application, ensuring high-quality, low-latency, and reliable AI responses.
- Work at the intersection of product and engineering to transform user requirements into functional AI-driven features.
- Implement Retrieval-Augmented Generation systems and fine-tune models to improve accuracy and context-awareness of agents.
