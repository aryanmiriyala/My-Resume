# Graduate Software Engineer - Fuze Services

Company: Fuze Health  
Location: Remote - United States  
Role: Graduate Software Engineer

## Company Context

At Fuze Health, we put patients first and tirelessly address the most pressing needs in healthcare. We empower millions to digitally connect with care providers, essential health resources and needed treatments, and enable care providers, employers, health plans and life sciences companies to meaningfully enhance quality, outcomes and value. We are dedicated to helping our partners evolve and modernize to meet emerging patient and marketplace needs.

Fuze Health's foundation is built upon the strategic combination of several proven, technology-powered innovators in the digital health, diagnostics, and pharmacy sectors. The portfolio brings together the capabilities of LetsGetChecked, Truepill, and Alto Pharmacy to create a unified force in healthcare.

## Role Summary

The role is an entry-level graduate software engineering opportunity in the US technology team. The engineer will work at the intersection of product engineering, cloud computing, and complex data environments, with emphasis on log/data analysis, troubleshooting, issue triage, bug fixing, feature development, documentation, and cross-functional communication.

## Responsibilities

- Review application logs, system metrics, and operational data to identify patterns, errors, and potential issues.
- Work with engineering teams to triage incoming bugs and support tickets.
- Provide initial findings and relevant information from log analysis.
- Support bug fixing and initial development of new features.
- Collaborate across Quality Assurance, Operations, and Engineering.
- Maintain clear documentation of issues, findings, and resolutions in the knowledge base.

## Requirements and Preferences

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field.
- Curiosity, problem solving, root-cause analysis, and automated testing discipline.
- Interest in software development and career growth in engineering.
- Ability to work autonomously on complex problems and adapt to changing priorities.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Familiarity with programming languages such as Python, C#, and JavaScript.
- Basic understanding of software systems.
- Desired: application observability tools such as Splunk or Datadog.
- Desired: cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or GCP.
