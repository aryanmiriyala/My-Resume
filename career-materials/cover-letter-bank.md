Use this file for reusable, truthful cover-letter paragraphs. Tailor every paragraph to the company and role before using it.

## Cover Letter Rules

- Ask Aryan about his personal connection to the company before drafting.
- Keep the letter concise and under one page.
- Do not repeat the resume; add motivation, context, and fit.
- Show company research and role-specific alignment.
- Use one or two concrete examples instead of broad claims.
- Keep the tone warm, professional, and human.
- Mention personal connection only when it is truthful and useful.
